## History

Niger was part of several West African empires and kingdoms, including Songhai. French colonization began in the late 19th century. Independence came on August 3, 1960. The country has experienced multiple coups, most recently in 2023, resulting in sanctions and tensions with France and regional neighbors.

## Economy

Niger is consistently ranked among the world's least developed countries. Uranium mining has been a key resource, though prices have been volatile. Agriculture and herding are the main livelihoods, heavily impacted by droughts and desertification. Population growth (the world's highest fertility rate) strains limited resources.
