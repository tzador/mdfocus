## Geography

Senegal surrounds The Gambia on three sides and has a diverse landscape: sandy Atlantic beaches, mangroves, the Sahel in the north, and forests in the south. The Senegal and Gambia rivers are important waterways. The Pink Lake (Lac Rose) is a notable natural attraction.

## Economy

Senegal has a diversified economy including agriculture (peanuts, fishing), tourism, mining (phosphates, gold), and services. Dakar is a major port and regional hub. The country is developing offshore oil and gas resources. Remittances from the diaspora are significant.

## Culture

Senegalese culture is renowned worldwide. Wolof is the most widely spoken language. The country has produced legendary musicians (Youssou N'Dour) and is the birthplace of mbalax music. Wrestling (laamb) is the national sport. Senegalese cuisine features dishes like thieboudienne.
