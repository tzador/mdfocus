## History

The Maya civilization flourished in this region. Britain claimed the area, known as British Honduras, in the 17th century. Independence came on September 21, 1981. Guatemala has maintained a territorial claim.

## Geography

Belize has Caribbean coast, jungle interior, and Maya Mountains. The Belize Barrier Reef is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The Great Blue Hole is a famous dive site.

## Economy

Tourism (reef, jungle, Maya ruins), agriculture (sugar, bananas), and fishing drive the economy. Offshore financial services exist but are limited.

## Culture

Belize's culture is diverse: Mestizo, Creole, Maya, Garifuna, and Mennonite communities coexist. Garifuna music and dance are UNESCO-recognized traditions.
