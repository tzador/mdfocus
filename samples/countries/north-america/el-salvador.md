## History

El Salvador was part of Spanish Central America until independence in 1821. A brutal civil war (1979-1992) killed 75,000. Post-war period saw high gang violence. President Nayib Bukele has reduced violence through controversial policies.

## Geography

El Salvador is the only Central American country without a Caribbean coast. The terrain is volcanic with Pacific beaches. Earthquakes and volcanic eruptions are risks.

## Economy

El Salvador has a developing economy with remittances being crucial (20%+ of GDP). Coffee, textiles, and tourism are important. Bitcoin adoption is an experiment.

## Culture

Salvadoran culture reflects its indigenous and Spanish heritage. Pupusas (stuffed corn cakes) are the national dish. Surf culture is growing on the Pacific coast.
