## History

The Kalinago (Carib) people resisted European colonization longer than most Caribbean islands. France and Britain fought over Dominica until British control was established. Independence came on November 3, 1978.

## Geography

Dominica is mountainous and volcanic, with hot springs, waterfalls, and the world's second-largest hot spring lake (Boiling Lake). Rainforests cover much of the island. Hurricane Maria devastated the island in 2017.

## Economy

Dominica has a small economy based on agriculture (bananas), eco-tourism, and citizenship by investment. Recovery from Hurricane Maria continues.

## Culture

Dominica has the only remaining Kalinago territory in the Caribbean. Creole culture blends African, French, and British influences. Traditional music includes bouyon and jing ping.
