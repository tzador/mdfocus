## History

Costa Rica was a Spanish colony, gaining independence in 1821. It abolished its military in 1948 after a brief civil war, redirecting resources to education and health. This unique decision has contributed to stability.

## Geography

Costa Rica has both Caribbean and Pacific coasts. The terrain includes volcanoes, rainforests, and cloud forests. About 25% of the country is protected. Biodiversity is extraordinary for its size.

## Economy

Costa Rica has the region's highest standard of living. Eco-tourism, technology (Intel, medical devices), and agriculture (coffee, bananas) drive the economy. The country aims for carbon neutrality.
