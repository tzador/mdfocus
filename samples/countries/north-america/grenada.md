## History

French and then British colonization shaped Grenada. Independence came in 1974. A Marxist coup and internal conflict led to the US invasion (Operation Urgent Fury) in 1983. Stability has since prevailed.

## Geography

Grenada includes the main island and the southern Grenadines. The terrain is volcanic with lush vegetation. The climate is tropical with hurricane risk.

## Economy

Nutmeg (Grenada produces a third of world supply), mace, and tourism drive the economy. Chocolate production is growing. Hurricane Ivan (2004) severely damaged the economy.

## Culture

Grenadian culture blends African, French, and British influences. Spicemas (Carnival) is the major festival. Grenadian cuisine features nutmeg in many dishes.
