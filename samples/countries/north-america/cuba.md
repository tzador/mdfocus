## History

Cuba was a Spanish colony until 1898, then under US influence until the 1959 revolution led by Fidel Castro. The revolution aligned Cuba with the Soviet Union, leading to the 1962 missile crisis. The embargo began in 1960.

## Geography

Cuba is the Caribbean's largest island, with fertile plains and small mountain ranges. The climate is tropical with hurricane risk.

## Economy

Cuba has a state-controlled economy, struggling under the US embargo. Tourism, remittances, sugar, and tobacco are key sectors. Medical services are exported. Chronic shortages affect daily life.

## Culture

Cuban culture is globally influential: son, salsa, and Buena Vista Social Club music; literature; baseball; cigars; and classic cars (preserved due to embargo) are iconic.
