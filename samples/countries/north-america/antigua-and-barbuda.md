## History

The islands were inhabited by indigenous peoples before Columbus arrived in 1493. English colonization began in 1632. Sugar plantations using enslaved Africans dominated the economy. Independence came on November 1, 1981.

## Geography

Antigua is the larger island with coral beaches. Barbuda is a flat coral island with a large frigate bird sanctuary. The climate is tropical with hurricane risk.

## Economy

Tourism dominates, with cruise ships and resort visitors. Financial services and online gaming are growing. The 2017 Hurricane Irma devastated Barbuda.

## Culture

Culture blends African and British heritage. Carnival is the major festival. Cricket is the national sport.
