## Geography

The Bahamas comprises over 700 islands spanning 800 km. Only about 30 are inhabited. The terrain is flat coral islands with some of the world's clearest water. Hurricane risk is significant.

## Economy

Tourism and financial services dominate. The country is a major offshore banking center. Cruise ships bring millions annually. Fishing is important for local consumption.

## Culture

Bahamian culture reflects African heritage with British colonial influence. Junkanoo festival features elaborate costumes and music. Conch dishes are signature cuisine.
