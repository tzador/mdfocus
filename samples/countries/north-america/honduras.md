## Geography

Honduras has extensive Caribbean coastline, the Bay Islands (popular for diving), and mountainous interior. Tropical forests and the Mosquito Coast are biodiverse regions.

## Economy

Honduras is one of the poorest Latin American countries. Textiles, coffee, bananas, and remittances are economic pillars. The Bay Islands attract tourists.

## Culture

Honduran culture blends Mestizo, indigenous (Lenca, Garifuna), and Caribbean influences. The Garifuna people on the coast have UNESCO-recognized traditions. Soccer is passionately followed.
