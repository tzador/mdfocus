## History

Trinidad was Spanish, then British, while Tobago changed hands multiple times. Large-scale immigration from India after slavery abolition shaped demographics. Independence came on August 31, 1962. The country became a republic in 1976.

## Geography

Trinidad is close to South America's coast, with diverse ecosystems including the Pitch Lake (natural asphalt). Tobago is smaller, more mountainous, and tourism-focused.

## Economy

Oil and gas dominate, making this the Caribbean's wealthiest nation. Manufacturing and petrochemicals are significant. Tobago relies on tourism.

## Culture

Trinidad invented steel pan (drums made from oil barrels) and is the birthplace of calypso and soca music. Carnival is world-famous. The population is roughly equally Afro-Trinidadian and Indo-Trinidadian.
