## History

Barbados was claimed by England in 1625 and was a sugar plantation colony using enslaved Africans. It was the wealthiest British Caribbean colony. Independence came in 1966. Barbados became a republic in November 2021.

## Geography

Barbados is a flat coral island with white sand beaches on the west and rougher Atlantic waters on the east. The climate is tropical but moderated by trade winds.

## Economy

Tourism is the main industry, with cruise ships and resorts. Financial services and light manufacturing are also important. Rum is a famous export.
