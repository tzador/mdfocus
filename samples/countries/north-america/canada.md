## History

Indigenous peoples inhabited Canada for thousands of years. French and British colonization shaped the nation. Confederation created Canada on July 1, 1867. The country gradually gained full independence, with the Constitution patriated in 1982.

## Geography

Canada spans six time zones with diverse landscapes: Rocky Mountains, prairies, Great Lakes, Arctic tundra, and extensive coastlines. The country has more lakes than the rest of the world combined.

## Economy

Canada has a highly developed economy with natural resources (oil, mining, forestry), manufacturing, and services. Trade with the United States dominates. Toronto and Vancouver are major financial centers.

## Culture

Canadian culture emphasizes multiculturalism and bilingualism. Ice hockey is the national passion. Indigenous cultures are increasingly recognized. Politeness and maple syrup are stereotypes with truth.
