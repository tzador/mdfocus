## History

The indigenous TaÃ­no people named the island "Xaymaca" (land of wood and water). Spanish then British colonization brought enslaved Africans. Independence came on August 6, 1962. Jamaica has remained stable but faces economic challenges and crime.

## Geography

Jamaica is mountainous, with Blue Mountains producing famous coffee. The island has numerous beaches and a tropical climate. Hurricanes are a risk.

## Economy

Tourism (resort beaches, cultural tourism) and bauxite mining are primary industries. Remittances are significant. The economy struggles with debt and inequality.

## Culture

Jamaica's cultural influence is enormous: reggae (Bob Marley), dancehall, Rastafari, jerk cuisine, and athletics (Usain Bolt). Patois is widely spoken alongside English.
