## History

Nicaragua was part of Spanish Central America until independence in 1821. US intervention marked the early 20th century. The Sandinista revolution (1979) overthrew the Somoza dictatorship. Civil war against US-backed Contras followed.

Daniel Ortega returned to power in 2007 and has consolidated authoritarian control, particularly since 2018 protests.

## Geography

Nicaragua has Lake Nicaragua (Central America's largest lake) and Lake Managua. The Pacific side is volcanic; the Caribbean coast is tropical lowland. Biodiversity is significant.

## Economy

Nicaragua is one of the poorest Latin American countries. Agriculture, manufacturing, and remittances drive the economy. Tourism has been affected by political instability.
