## History

The islands were among the first Caribbean colonies (British from 1623). Sugar plantations dominated the economy. Independence came on September 19, 1983. Nevis has the constitutional right to secede.

## Geography

Both islands are volcanic. Saint Kitts is larger; Nevis is separated by a narrow strait. The climate is tropical with hurricane risk.

## Economy

Tourism and financial services (including citizenship by investment) drive the economy. Former sugar industry declined but is part of heritage tourism.

## Culture

Culture blends African and British heritage. Carnival and music festivals are celebrated. Cricket is popular. Historic Brimstone Hill Fortress is a UNESCO site.
