## History

Panama separated from Colombia in 1903 with US support to facilitate canal construction. The US controlled the Canal Zone until 1999. The canal fully transferred to Panama on December 31, 1999.

The US invaded in 1989 to remove dictator Manuel Noriega.

## Geography

Panama is an S-shaped isthmus with Caribbean and Pacific coasts. The Panama Canal cuts through. Rainforests and biodiversity are significant. The climate is tropical.

## Economy

The Panama Canal generates significant revenue. Banking, shipping registration (largest in the world), and free trade zones drive the economy. The country is a major logistics hub.

## Culture

Panamanian culture blends indigenous, African, Spanish, and American influences. The Panama hat actually originated in Ecuador but is associated with Panama. The pollera is the traditional female costume.
