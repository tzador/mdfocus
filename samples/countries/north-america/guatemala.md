## History

Guatemala was the center of Maya civilization and later the seat of Spanish colonial power in Central America. Independence came in 1821. A 36-year civil war (1960-1996) killed over 200,000, with Maya communities particularly affected.

## Geography

Guatemala has volcanic highlands, Pacific coast, Caribbean coast, and lowland jungles (PetÃ©n) where Maya cities like Tikal are found. Volcanic eruptions and earthquakes are significant risks.

## Economy

Guatemala has Central America's largest economy, though wealth is very unequally distributed. Coffee, sugar, bananas, and textiles are key exports. Remittances are crucial.

## Culture

Guatemalan culture blends Maya and Spanish heritage. About 40% of the population is indigenous. Traditional textiles, particularly huipiles, are distinctive. Semana Santa (Holy Week) celebrations are elaborate.
