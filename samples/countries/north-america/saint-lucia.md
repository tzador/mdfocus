## History

The island changed hands between France and Britain 14 times, finally becoming British in 1814. This history created a French Creole cultural influence despite English rule. Independence came on February 22, 1979.

## Geography

Saint Lucia is volcanic, with the famous twin Pitons rising dramatically. Rainforests, beaches, and the world's only drive-in volcano (Sulphur Springs) attract tourists.

## Economy

Tourism dominates, with cruise ships and resort visitors. Banana exports declined but remain significant. The island is developing luxury tourism.
