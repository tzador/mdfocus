## History

France has been a major European power since medieval times. The 1789 French Revolution transformed global politics. France had a vast colonial empire. It was occupied in WWII but liberated in 1944.

France is a founding EU member and a permanent UN Security Council member with nuclear weapons.

## Geography

France is the largest Western European country, with diverse landscapes: Alps, Pyrenees, Atlantic and Mediterranean coasts, and fertile plains. Overseas territories span the globe.

## Economy

France has the world's seventh-largest economy. Key sectors include aerospace, pharmaceuticals, fashion, and agriculture. Tourism (80+ million visitors annually) is a major industry.

## Culture

French culture has profoundly influenced art, literature, philosophy, and cuisine globally. Paris is a cultural capital. French cinema, fashion, and wine are world-renowned.
