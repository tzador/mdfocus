## History

Finland was part of Sweden for centuries, then a Russian Grand Duchy until independence in 1917. The Winter War (1939-1940) against the Soviet Union shaped national identity.

Finland maintained neutrality during the Cold War but joined the EU in 1995 and NATO in 2023 following Russia's invasion of Ukraine.

## Geography

Finland has thousands of lakes and extensive forests covering 70% of the land. Lapland in the north experiences the midnight sun and northern lights. The climate is cold continental.

## Economy

Finland has a highly developed economy known for technology (Nokia), forestry, and engineering. The education system is globally admired. Social welfare is extensive.

## Culture

Finnish culture values nature, sauna culture, and design. The Kalevala epic poem is a national treasure. Finnish music ranges from classical (Sibelius) to heavy metal. Santa Claus Village is in Lapland.
