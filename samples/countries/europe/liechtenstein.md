## History

Liechtenstein has been independent since 1719, named after the Liechtenstein family who purchased the territory. It remained neutral in both World Wars and developed into a prosperous financial center.

The principality has a customs union with Switzerland and uses the Swiss franc.

## Economy

Liechtenstein has one of the world's highest GDPs per capita. Financial services, manufacturing (dental products, ceramics), and tourism are key sectors. There is no airport or train station.

## Culture

Liechtenstein shares Germanic culture with its neighbors. The Prince's castle overlooks Vaduz. The country has a high quality of life and hosts cultural events despite its small size.
