## History

The Dutch Republic was a major trading and colonial power in the 17th-century Golden Age. The Kingdom of the Netherlands was established in 1815. The country was occupied by Nazi Germany in WWII.

The Netherlands was a founding member of the EU, NATO, and UN.

## Geography

Much of the Netherlands is below sea level, protected by dikes and polders. The country is flat with major rivers (Rhine, Meuse). The climate is temperate maritime.

## Economy

The Netherlands has a highly developed economy with agriculture (flowers, dairy), manufacturing, logistics (Port of Rotterdam), and services. It is a major trading nation and hosts many multinationals.

## Culture

Dutch culture values tolerance, pragmatism, and cycling. The country has produced renowned painters (Rembrandt, Van Gogh, Vermeer). Liberal policies on cannabis and euthanasia are distinctive.
