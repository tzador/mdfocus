## History

The Grand Duchy of Lithuania was one of medieval Europe's largest states. It merged with Poland before Russian annexation. Brief independence (1918-1940) ended with Soviet occupation.

Independence was restored in 1990 (the first Soviet republic to declare independence). Lithuania joined NATO and the EU in 2004.

## Geography

Lithuania is mostly flat, with forests, lakes, and Baltic Sea coast. The Curonian Spit is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The climate is humid continental.

## Culture

Lithuanian culture preserves ancient Baltic traditions and the Lithuanian language, one of the world's oldest surviving Indo-European languages. Basketball is a national passion.
