## History

Moldova (Bessarabia) was part of Romania between the wars, then a Soviet republic. Independence came in 1991. Transnistria, backed by Russian troops, operates as an unrecognized breakaway state.

Moldova has EU candidate status and seeks European integration.

## Geography

Moldova is mostly rolling hills and fertile plains. The country is known for its wine regions. The climate is temperate continental.

## Economy

Moldova is Europe's poorest country. Agriculture (especially wine), remittances, and services are key sectors. Transnistria complicates governance.

## Culture

Moldovan culture is Romanian, with wine culture particularly important. The country has one of the world's highest concentrations of vineyards. Orthodox Christianity is the main religion.
