## History

Russia has a long history from the Kievan Rus' through the Tsarist Empire. The 1917 Revolution established the Soviet Union, which collapsed in 1991. Vladimir Putin has dominated Russian politics since 1999.

Russia's 2022 full-scale invasion of Ukraine has led to unprecedented Western sanctions and isolation.

## Geography

Russia spans 11 time zones, from the Baltic to the Pacific. Terrain includes European plains, Siberian taiga, tundra, and mountains. Lake Baikal is the world's deepest lake.

## Economy

Russia has a large economy based on oil, gas, and mineral exports. Sanctions have impacted trade and technology access. The economy is increasingly oriented toward China and non-Western markets.

## Culture

Russian culture has contributed enormously to literature (Tolstoy, Dostoevsky), music (Tchaikovsky), and ballet. Orthodox Christianity is the main religion. Russian is widely spoken across the former Soviet Union.
