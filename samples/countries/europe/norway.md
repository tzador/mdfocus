## History

Norway was a major Viking power. It was in unions with Denmark, then Sweden, until full independence in 1905. The country was occupied by Nazi Germany in WWII.

Norway has rejected EU membership twice (1972, 1994) but is in the EEA. North Sea oil transformed the economy from the 1970s.

## Geography

Norway has dramatic fjords, mountains, and glaciers. The coastline is extremely long due to fjord indentations. The Arctic archipelago of Svalbard and Jan Mayen are Norwegian. The midnight sun and polar nights are phenomena of the north.

## Economy

Norway has one of the world's highest GDPs per capita. Oil and gas wealth is managed through the Government Pension Fund Global, the world's largest sovereign wealth fund. Fishing and shipping remain important.

## Culture

Norwegian culture values nature and outdoor activities. Edvard Munch and Henrik Ibsen are cultural icons. The Nobel Peace Prize is awarded in Oslo. Skiing is a national tradition.
