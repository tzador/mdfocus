## History

Malta has been inhabited since 5900 BCE, with famous megalithic temples. The Knights of St. John ruled from 1530 to 1798. British rule lasted until independence in 1964.

Malta joined the EU in 2004 and the Eurozone in 2008.

## Geography

Malta consists of three inhabited islands: Malta, Gozo, and Comino. The terrain is rocky and low-lying with no permanent rivers. The climate is Mediterranean.

## Economy

Malta's economy relies on tourism, financial services, and iGaming. The country has attracted online gaming companies. Ship registration is significant. English language schools draw students.
