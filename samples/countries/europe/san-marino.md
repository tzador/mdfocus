## History

San Marino claims to be the world's oldest surviving sovereign state and constitutional republic, founded by a Christian stonemason fleeing Roman persecution. It has maintained independence through diplomacy.

## Geography

San Marino is landlocked and entirely surrounded by Italy. Mount Titano dominates the small territory. The climate is Mediterranean.

## Economy

San Marino's economy relies on tourism, banking, and manufacturing (ceramics, wine). The country uses the euro through an agreement with the EU.

## Culture

Sammarinese culture is Italian-influenced. The three towers on Mount Titano are the national symbol. Traditional festivals celebrate the republic's heritage.
