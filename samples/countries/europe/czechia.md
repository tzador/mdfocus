## History

The lands of Bohemia and Moravia have a rich history including the medieval Kingdom of Bohemia. The region was part of the Habsburg Empire, then Czechoslovakia after WWI. Communist rule followed WWII.

The Velvet Revolution in 1989 ended communism peacefully. Czechoslovakia peacefully split into Czechia and Slovakia in 1993.

## Geography

Czechia is surrounded by mountains, with the Bohemian Basin in the center. Major rivers include the Vltava and Elbe. The climate is temperate continental.

## Economy

Czechia has one of the most developed economies in Central Europe. Manufacturing (particularly automobiles), engineering, and services are key sectors. It has not adopted the euro.

## Culture

Czech culture is known for literature (Kafka, Kundera), music, and beer culture (highest consumption per capita globally). Prague's historic architecture draws millions of tourists.
