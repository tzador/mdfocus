## History

Romania was formed from the principalities of Wallachia and Moldavia in 1859. It gained full independence in 1878. After WWII, communist rule under CeauÈ™escu was particularly harsh until the 1989 revolution.

Romania joined NATO in 2004 and the EU in 2007.

## Geography

Romania has diverse landscapes: the Carpathian Mountains curve through the country, with the Transylvanian Plateau inside. The Danube forms the southern border and flows into the Black Sea through a delta.

## Economy

Romania has one of the fastest-growing EU economies, with IT services, automotive manufacturing, and agriculture as key sectors. The IT sector is particularly strong.

## Culture

Romanian culture is Latin-based, distinct from Slavic neighbors. Transylvania's association with Dracula attracts tourists. Orthodox Christianity, folk traditions, and medieval architecture are important.
