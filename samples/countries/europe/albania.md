## History

Albania has ancient Illyrian heritage. Ottoman rule lasted from the 15th century until independence in 1912. After WWII, Enver Hoxha imposed one of the world's most isolated communist regimes until 1991.

The transition to democracy was turbulent, including a 1997 crisis triggered by pyramid schemes. Albania has since stabilized and pursues EU integration.

## Economy

Albania has transitioned to a market economy, with services, agriculture, and textiles as key sectors. Tourism is growing rapidly. Remittances from the diaspora are significant.

## Culture

Albanian culture is unique, with its own language unrelated to neighbors. Traditional music includes iso-polyphony (UNESCO recognized). The country has a mix of Muslim, Orthodox, and Catholic populations living harmoniously.
