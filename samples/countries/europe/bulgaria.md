## History

The First Bulgarian Empire was a major medieval power. Ottoman rule lasted nearly 500 years until independence in 1878. Bulgaria was a communist state after WWII until 1990.

Bulgaria joined NATO in 2004 and the EU in 2007 but faces challenges with corruption and population decline.

## Geography

Bulgaria has diverse terrain: the Danube plain, Balkan Mountains, and Black Sea coast. The climate is continental with Mediterranean influences in the south.

## Economy

Bulgaria has a developing market economy, one of the poorest in the EU. Manufacturing, IT services, and tourism are growing sectors. The country aims to join the Eurozone.

## Culture

Bulgarian culture features distinctive music (including complex rhythms), Orthodox Christianity, and Cyrillic script (which originated here). Rose oil production and yogurt are famous exports.
