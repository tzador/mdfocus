## History

Estonia was ruled by Germans, Danes, Swedes, and Russians before brief independence (1918-1940). Soviet annexation lasted until 1991's Singing Revolution restored independence.

Estonia joined NATO and the EU in 2004, rapidly developing its digital infrastructure and economy.

## Geography

Estonia is flat, with extensive forests, wetlands, and over 2,000 islands. The Baltic Sea coast is extensive. The climate is humid continental.

## Economy

Estonia has developed a dynamic technology sector, with companies like Skype originating here. E-residency and digital government services are pioneering. The economy is service-oriented.

## Culture

Estonian culture includes unique traditions, a Finno-Ugric language related to Finnish, and the Song Festival tradition (UNESCO recognized). Tallinn's medieval Old Town is a UNESCO site.
