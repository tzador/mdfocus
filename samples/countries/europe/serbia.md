## History

The medieval Serbian kingdom was powerful before Ottoman conquest. Serbia was the core of Yugoslavia (1918-2003). The 1990s saw wars as Yugoslavia dissolved, with NATO bombing Serbia in 1999 over Kosovo.

Serbia is an EU candidate but maintains close ties with Russia.

## Geography

Serbia is mostly landlocked plains (Vojvodina) and mountains. The Danube and Sava rivers meet at Belgrade. The climate is continental.

## Economy

Serbia has a developing economy with services, manufacturing, and agriculture. Belgrade is a regional hub. Foreign investment has grown, though EU integration is delayed.

## Culture

Serbian culture is Orthodox Christian and Slavic. Traditional music (including turbo-folk), medieval monasteries, and sporting achievements (tennis, especially Djokovic) are notable.
