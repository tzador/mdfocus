## History

Switzerland's confederation dates to 1291. Neutrality has been maintained since 1815, including through both World Wars. The country has stayed outside the EU but has extensive bilateral agreements.

## Geography

The Alps cover much of Switzerland, with major peaks including the Matterhorn. Lakes and valleys dot the landscape. The climate is Alpine and temperate.
