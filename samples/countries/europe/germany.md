## History

Germany unified in 1871 under Prussia. It fought both World Wars, with the Nazi regime committing the Holocaust. Post-WWII division ended with reunification in 1990.

Germany is the EU's largest member and a key NATO ally, though constitutionally limited in military deployment.

## Geography

Germany spans from the North Sea and Baltic coasts to the Alps. Major rivers include the Rhine and Danube. The landscape includes forests, plains, and low mountains.

## Economy

Germany has Europe's largest and world's fourth-largest economy. Automotive (BMW, Mercedes, Volkswagen), machinery, and chemicals dominate. The Mittelstand (medium-sized companies) is an economic backbone.

## Culture

German culture has contributed enormously to philosophy, music (Beethoven, Bach), literature, and science. Beer culture and festivals (Oktoberfest) are famous. Football is a national passion.
