## History

Spain was unified in 1469 and built a vast empire in the Americas. Civil War (1936-1939) led to Franco's dictatorship until 1975. Democracy was restored under King Juan Carlos.

Spain joined the EU in 1986. The Catalan independence movement has been a major issue.

## Geography

Spain is mostly a high plateau (Meseta) with mountains (Pyrenees, Sierra Nevada). It has Atlantic and Mediterranean coasts. The Canary and Balearic Islands are Spanish. The climate varies from oceanic to Mediterranean to semi-arid.

## Economy

Spain has the fourth-largest EU economy. Tourism, automotive, and agriculture are key sectors. The 2008 crisis hit hard, with slow recovery.
