## History

Austria was the center of the Habsburg Empire for centuries, ruling much of Europe. The Austro-Hungarian Empire collapsed after WWI. Austria was annexed by Nazi Germany (Anschluss) in 1938.

After WWII occupation, Austria declared permanent neutrality in 1955. It joined the EU in 1995.

## Geography

Austria is predominantly mountainous, with the Alps covering much of the west. The Danube River flows through the north. The climate is continental, with cold winters and mild summers.

## Economy

Austria has a highly developed social market economy. Tourism, manufacturing (machinery, vehicles), and services are key sectors. Vienna is a major international city hosting UN and OPEC headquarters.

## Culture

Austrian culture has global influence in classical music (Mozart, Haydn, Strauss), art, and architecture. Vienna's coffeehouse culture and the Viennese Waltz are distinctive. Alpine traditions and winter sports are important.
