## History

The region has ancient history connected to Alexander the Great's Macedonia (though the connection is disputed). It was part of the Ottoman Empire, then Yugoslavia. Independence came peacefully in 1991.

The Prespa Agreement with Greece (2019) changed the name from "Macedonia," allowing NATO membership that year.

## Geography

North Macedonia is mountainous, with Lake Ohrid (UNESCO site) on the Albanian border. The Vardar River flows through Skopje. The climate is continental.

## Economy

North Macedonia is a developing economy with manufacturing, services, and agriculture. EU candidate status since 2005 awaits progress on reforms. Remittances are important.

## Culture

Macedonian culture is South Slavic and Orthodox Christian. Ohrid's medieval churches are significant. Traditional music and cuisine reflect Balkan influences.
