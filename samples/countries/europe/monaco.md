## History

The Grimaldi family has ruled Monaco since 1297. The principality has treaties with France for protection. The Monte Carlo Casino, opened in 1863, transformed Monaco into a luxury destination.

## Geography

Monaco is entirely urban, squeezed between the Alps and Mediterranean. Land reclamation has expanded the tiny territory. The climate is Mediterranean.

## Economy

Monaco has no income tax, attracting the wealthy. Tourism, banking, and hosting events (Grand Prix, yacht shows) drive the economy. The country has the highest GDP per capita in the world.
