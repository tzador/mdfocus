## History

The UK was formed by the union of England, Scotland, Wales, and Ireland (later Northern Ireland). The British Empire was the largest in history. The UK was central to the Industrial Revolution and both World Wars.

Brexit (2020) marked the UK's departure from the EU after 47 years of membership.

## Geography

The UK comprises the island of Great Britain plus Northern Ireland. The terrain includes the Scottish Highlands, English lowlands, and Welsh mountains. The climate is temperate maritime.

## Economy

The UK has the world's sixth-largest economy. Financial services (London), pharmaceuticals, and aerospace are key sectors. Brexit has created economic adjustments.

## Culture

British culture has global influence through the English language, literature (Shakespeare, Dickens), music (The Beatles, Adele), and media (BBC). Football, cricket, and rugby are major sports.
