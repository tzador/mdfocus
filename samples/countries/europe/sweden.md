## History

Sweden was a major power in the 17th-18th centuries. It has been at peace since 1814, the longest unbroken peace in Europe. Social democratic governments built the welfare state. Sweden remained neutral in both World Wars.

Sweden joined the EU in 1995 but keeps its currency. Russia's invasion of Ukraine led to NATO membership in 2024.

## Geography

Sweden is largely forested with many lakes. The north extends above the Arctic Circle. The Baltic Sea coast features the Stockholm archipelago.

## Economy

Sweden has a highly developed economy with strong technology, manufacturing, and services sectors. Companies like Spotify, IKEA, and Volvo are Swedish. The social model balances high taxes with extensive welfare.

## Culture

Swedish culture emphasizes equality, nature, and design. Nobel Prizes are awarded in Stockholm. ABBA, Ingmar Bergman, and modern pop music have global reach. Midsummer is the main holiday.
