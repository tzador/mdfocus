## History

Ancient Greece laid foundations for Western civilization. The Byzantine Empire continued Greek culture. Ottoman rule lasted until independence in 1821. The 20th century included dictatorship and democratic restoration.

Greece joined the EU in 1981 and the Eurozone in 2001, experiencing a severe debt crisis from 2009.

## Geography

Greece is mountainous with thousands of islands in the Aegean and Ionian seas. The mainland includes the Peloponnese peninsula. The Mediterranean climate features hot, dry summers.

## Economy

Greece has a developed economy based on shipping, tourism, and services. The 2009-2018 debt crisis was severe. Tourism and maritime shipping are key sectors.

## Culture

Greek culture has influenced global civilization through philosophy, arts, and sciences. Greek Orthodox Christianity is central to identity. Mediterranean cuisine and traditional music are distinctive.
