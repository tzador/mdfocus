## History

Ukraine has ancient history as the center of Kievan Rus'. It was divided between various powers before brief independence (1918). Soviet Ukraine existed until 1991. The 2014 Euromaidan revolution ousted a pro-Russian president, leading to Russia's annexation of Crimea and war in Donbas.

Russia's full-scale invasion in February 2022 began Europe's largest war since WWII.

## Geography

Ukraine is mostly steppe and plain, with the Carpathian Mountains in the west. The Dnieper River divides the country. The Black Sea coast includes Crimea (occupied by Russia). The climate is temperate continental.

## Economy

Ukraine was an agricultural and industrial power. War has devastated the economy. Ukraine is a major grain exporter and had significant manufacturing and IT sectors.

## Culture

Ukrainian culture is distinct, with its own language, Orthodox and Greek Catholic traditions, and folk arts. Ukrainian literature, music, and cuisine are rich. National identity has strengthened dramatically during the war.
