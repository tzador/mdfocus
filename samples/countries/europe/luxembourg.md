## History

Luxembourg has been a county, duchy, and grand duchy since medieval times. Despite occupations, it has maintained independence. It was a founding member of the EU, NATO, and UN.

## Geography

Luxembourg is mostly rolling hills and forests. The Ardennes cover the north. The country borders Belgium, France, and Germany.

## Economy

Luxembourg has one of the world's highest GDPs per capita. Finance, steel, and EU institutions drive the economy. Many cross-border workers commute daily. Banking secrecy has been reformed.

## Culture

Luxembourgish culture blends French and German influences. The country's multilingualism is remarkable. Luxembourg City's Old Town is a UNESCO site.
