## History

Slovakia was part of Hungary for a millennium before Czechoslovakia was created in 1918. After communist rule, the Velvet Divorce in 1993 peacefully separated Slovakia from Czechia.

Slovakia joined NATO and the EU in 2004, adopting the euro in 2009.

## Geography

Slovakia is mountainous, with the High Tatras being the highest peaks. The Danube forms part of the southern border. The climate is temperate.

## Economy

Slovakia has a developed economy with strong automotive manufacturing (Volkswagen, Kia, Peugeot). The country is called the "Detroit of Europe" for car production per capita.

## Culture

Slovak culture is Slavic and Catholic. Folk traditions, castles, and natural beauty attract tourists. The capital Bratislava is close to Vienna and Budapest.
