## History

Montenegro resisted Ottoman conquest longer than neighbors. It was an independent kingdom before joining Yugoslavia. Following Yugoslavia's breakup, Montenegro was federated with Serbia until a 2006 independence referendum.

Montenegro joined NATO in 2017 and is negotiating EU membership.

## Geography

Montenegro has dramatic landscapes: Adriatic coast, mountains (including the Durmitor range), and the Bay of Kotor (Europe's southernmost fjord). The climate is Mediterranean on the coast, continental inland.

## Economy

Montenegro's economy depends heavily on tourism and services. The country uses the euro without EU membership. Aluminum and agriculture are other sectors. The country is popular with Russian investors.

## Culture

Montenegrin culture blends Orthodox, Mediterranean, and Balkan elements. The old town of Kotor is a UNESCO site. Traditional music and cuisine reflect diverse influences.
