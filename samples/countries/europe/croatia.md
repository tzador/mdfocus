## History

Croatia was a medieval kingdom before joining Hungary and later the Habsburg Empire. It became part of Yugoslavia after WWI. The Croatian War of Independence (1991-1995) established the modern state.

Croatia joined NATO in 2009, the EU in 2013, and adopted the euro in 2023.

## Geography

Croatia has a distinctive shape, with continental interior and extensive Adriatic coastline including thousands of islands. The Dinaric Alps run through the country. Plitvice Lakes are a UNESCO site.

## Economy

Tourism is Croatia's primary industry, drawing visitors to Dubrovnik, Split, and coastal islands. Shipbuilding, food processing, and pharmaceuticals are other important sectors.

## Culture

Croatian culture blends Central European and Mediterranean influences. Dubrovnik's Old Town is a UNESCO site featured in Game of Thrones. Traditional music includes klapa (a cappella) singing.
