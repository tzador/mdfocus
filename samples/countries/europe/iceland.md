## History

Iceland was settled by Norse Vikings around 870 CE. The Althing, established in 930, is one of the oldest parliaments. The country was under Norwegian, then Danish rule until independence in 1944.

The 2008 banking crisis severely impacted Iceland, but recovery was remarkable.

## Geography

Iceland sits on the Mid-Atlantic Ridge, with active volcanoes, geysers, glaciers, and hot springs. The landscape is stark and beautiful. The climate is subarctic but moderated by the Gulf Stream.

## Economy

Iceland has a highly developed economy based on fishing, tourism, and renewable energy. Geothermal and hydropower provide almost all electricity. Aluminum smelting uses cheap energy.

## Culture

Icelandic culture preserves Old Norse language and literature (sagas). The country has high literacy and strong arts scene. The Northern Lights and midnight sun draw tourists.
