## History

Latvia was ruled by Germans, Poles, Swedes, and Russians before brief independence (1918-1940). Soviet annexation lasted until independence was restored in 1991.

Latvia joined NATO and the EU in 2004, adopting the euro in 2014.

## Geography

Latvia is mostly flat, with extensive forests, rivers, and Baltic Sea coast. The Daugava River flows through Riga. The climate is humid continental.

## Economy

Latvia has a developed economy with services, manufacturing, and transit as key sectors. Riga is a regional hub. Population decline through emigration is a concern.

## Culture

Latvian culture features distinctive folk traditions, the Latvian Song and Dance Festival (UNESCO), and Art Nouveau architecture in Riga. The Latvian language is one of two surviving Baltic languages.
