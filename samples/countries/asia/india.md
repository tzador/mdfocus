## History

Indian civilization dates back over 5,000 years, including the Indus Valley Civilization. Major empires included Maurya, Gupta, and Mughal. British colonial rule lasted from 1858 until independence in 1947, accompanied by partition creating Pakistan.

India has developed as a diverse democracy, with Nehru, Indira Gandhi, and more recently Narendra Modi as influential leaders. Tensions with Pakistan and China remain.

## Geography

India spans from the Himalayas in the north to tropical coasts in the south. Major rivers include the Ganges, Brahmaputra, and Indus. The country has diverse climates, from deserts to rainforests. The monsoon is crucial for agriculture.

## Economy

India has the world's fifth-largest economy, with IT services, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing as key sectors. It has a large middle class but also significant poverty. Economic liberalization since 1991 has driven growth.

## Culture

Indian culture is extraordinarily diverse, with multiple religions (Hinduism, Islam, Buddhism, Sikhism), languages, and traditions. Bollywood is the world's largest film industry. Yoga, classical dance, and cuisine have global influence.
