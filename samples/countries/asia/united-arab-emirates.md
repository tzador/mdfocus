## History

The region was known for pearling and trade before British protection established the Trucial States. The UAE was formed on December 2, 1971, when six emirates united (Ras al-Khaimah joined in 1972).

Oil discovery transformed the federation. Dubai developed as a trade and tourism hub, while Abu Dhabi holds most oil reserves.

## Geography

The UAE is mostly desert, with a Persian Gulf coastline. The Empty Quarter borders the south. The climate is hot and arid. Dubai and Abu Dhabi have dramatically developed their coastlines.

## Economy

The UAE has successfully diversified from oil. Dubai is a global hub for finance, tourism, and logistics. Emirates and Etihad are major airlines. Abu Dhabi maintains sovereign wealth funds worth hundreds of billions.

## Culture

Emirati culture blends Bedouin traditions with cosmopolitan modernity. Islam is the official religion. The population is majority expatriate, creating diverse cultural influences. Traditional heritage is preserved alongside futuristic architecture.
