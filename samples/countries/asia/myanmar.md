## History

Burma was a powerful kingdom before British colonization in the 19th century. Independence came in 1948. Military rule began in 1962 and continued with brief democratic openings.

Aung San Suu Kyi led democratic opposition for decades. Democratic reforms (2011-2021) were reversed by a 2021 military coup. Civil war and resistance continue. The Rohingya crisis drew international condemnation.

## Geography

Myanmar has diverse terrain: coastal regions, river valleys (Irrawaddy), and mountainous borders. It has significant natural resources including jade, gems, and natural gas. The climate is tropical monsoon.

## Economy

Myanmar has significant resources but remains underdeveloped due to mismanagement and conflict. Agriculture employs most of the population. Sanctions and instability have limited foreign investment. The informal economy, including illicit drugs, is substantial.

## Culture

Burmese culture is influenced by Theravada Buddhism, with elaborate pagodas including Shwedagon. Traditional arts include puppetry, lacquerware, and dance. The longyi (wrapped garment) is traditional dress. Diverse ethnic groups have distinct cultures.
