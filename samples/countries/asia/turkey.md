## Geography

Turkey bridges Europe and Asia, with the Bosphorus and Dardanelles straits connecting the Black Sea to the Mediterranean. Terrain includes coastal areas, the Anatolian plateau, and eastern mountains. The country is seismically active.

## Economy

Turkey has a large, diversified economy with strong manufacturing, agriculture, and tourism sectors. Economic instability, including high inflation and currency depreciation, has been a recent challenge. Istanbul is a major economic hub.

## Culture

Turkish culture blends Central Asian, Middle Eastern, and Mediterranean influences. The country has world-class cuisine, music, and arts. Ottoman heritage is visible in architecture and traditions. Turkey's geopolitical position gives it unique cultural complexity.
