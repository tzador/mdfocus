## History

Phoenicia, ancient Lebanon, was a major trading civilization. The region has been ruled by various empires. Modern Lebanon emerged from French mandate and gained independence in 1943.

A devastating civil war (1975-1990) divided the country. Political instability, Syrian involvement, and conflicts with Israel have continued. A massive 2020 port explosion and economic collapse have created ongoing crisis.

## Geography

Lebanon is small but diverse, with Mediterranean coast, the Mount Lebanon range, the Bekaa Valley, and Anti-Lebanon Mountains. Cedars of Lebanon are a national symbol. The climate is Mediterranean with snowy winters in mountains.

## Economy

Lebanon's economy has collapsed since 2019, with currency devaluation and banking crisis. Historically, banking, tourism, and services were key sectors. Beirut was a regional financial hub. Recovery faces political gridlock.

## Culture

Lebanese culture is diverse, with Christian, Sunni, Shia, and Druze communities. The country has produced influential writers, artists, and musicians. Lebanese cuisine (mezze, hummus, tabbouleh) is renowned globally. Beirut was known for nightlife before the crisis.
