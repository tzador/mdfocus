## History

The House of Saud has ruled the Arabian Peninsula since the 18th century, with the modern kingdom founded in 1932. Oil discovery in 1938 transformed the country.

King Salman and Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman (MBS) have pursued Vision 2030 reforms while maintaining strict control. The murder of journalist Jamal Khashoggi drew international condemnation.

## Geography

Saudi Arabia is mostly desert, including part of the Empty Quarter (Rub' al Khali). The Hejaz region along the Red Sea contains Mecca and Medina. The climate is hot and arid.

## Economy

Saudi Arabia has the world's second-largest proven oil reserves. Oil revenues fund development and a generous welfare state. Vision 2030 aims to diversify through tourism, entertainment, and technology. NEOM is a planned futuristic mega-city.

## Culture

Saudi culture is conservative and Islamic. The kingdom is the custodian of Islam's two holiest mosques. Wahhabism has influenced the strict interpretation of Islam. Recent reforms have allowed entertainment, women driving, and tourism.
