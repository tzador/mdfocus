## History

Iran has a 2,500-year continuous history, with the Persian Empire under Cyrus the Great, Darius, and later dynasties. The Islamic conquest in the 7th century brought Shia Islam, which became dominant under the Safavids.

The Pahlavi dynasty modernized Iran before the 1979 Islamic Revolution established theocratic rule under Ayatollah Khomeini. The Iran-Iraq War (1980-1988) was devastating. International tensions over the nuclear program continue.

## Geography

Iran is a large, mountainous country with the Zagros and Alborz ranges. The interior is arid plateau. The country has Caspian Sea coastline in the north and Persian Gulf coast in the south. Iran borders seven countries.

## Economy

Iran has one of the world's largest oil and gas reserves. Sanctions have severely impacted the economy, causing high inflation and unemployment. The country has developed manufacturing and agriculture despite isolation.

## Culture

Persian culture has profoundly influenced the Islamic world and beyond. Poetry (Rumi, Hafez, Ferdowsi) is central to identity. Persian New Year (Nowruz), carpets, and cuisine are distinctive. Cinema has gained international acclaim.
