## Economy

Cambodia is a developing economy based on garments, tourism, and agriculture. Angkor Wat is a major tourist destination. The country has attracted manufacturing investment due to low labor costs. Corruption and weak institutions remain challenges.

## Culture

Khmer culture centers on Theravada Buddhism and the legacy of Angkor. Classical Apsara dance is a UNESCO tradition. Rice is the staple food. Despite the trauma of the Khmer Rouge, traditional arts and customs have been revived.
