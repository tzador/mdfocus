## History

Vietnam has millennia of history, often resisting Chinese domination. France colonized Indochina in the 19th century. After WWII, Ho Chi Minh declared independence, leading to wars against France and then the United States.

The Vietnam War ended in 1975 with communist reunification. Economic reforms (ƒê·ªïi M·ªõi) from 1986 transformed the economy while maintaining one-party rule.

## Geography

Vietnam is long and narrow, stretching along the eastern Indochina coast. Major features include the Red River Delta, Mekong Delta, and central highlands. The climate is tropical monsoon.

## Economy

Vietnam has achieved rapid economic growth, becoming a major manufacturing hub for electronics, textiles, and footwear. Agriculture (rice, coffee) remains important. The country has attracted significant foreign investment.
