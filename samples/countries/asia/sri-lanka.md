## History

Sri Lanka has over 2,000 years of recorded history, with Buddhist civilization and advanced hydraulic engineering. Portugal, Dutch, and British colonized successively. Independence came in 1948.

A civil war between the government and Tamil Tigers (1983-2009) killed over 100,000. An economic crisis in 2022 led to the president's ouster and ongoing difficulties.

## Geography

Sri Lanka is a teardrop-shaped island south of India. The terrain includes central highlands, coastal plains, and rainforests. The climate is tropical, influenced by monsoons. The country has remarkable biodiversity.

## Economy

Sri Lanka has a developing economy based on tourism, tea, textiles, and remittances. The 2022 economic collapse caused debt default, inflation, and shortages. IMF assistance is supporting recovery.

## Culture

Sri Lankan culture reflects its Sinhalese, Tamil, and colonial heritage. Theravada Buddhism dominates among Sinhalese, while Tamils are mostly Hindu. Ancient cities (Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa) and temples are UNESCO sites. Ceylon tea is world-famous.
