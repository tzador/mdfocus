## History

Melanesians settled the islands over 30,000 years ago. Britain colonized in the 19th century. WWII saw major battles, especially Guadalcanal. Independence came on July 7, 1978. Ethnic tensions led to Australian-led intervention (2003-2017).

## Geography

The Solomon Islands comprise six major islands and hundreds of smaller ones. The terrain is mountainous and forested. The climate is tropical.

## Economy

Logging, fishing, and agriculture are the main economic activities. Palm oil and copra are exports. Development is limited. The country switched recognition from Taiwan to China in 2019.

## Culture

Melanesian culture with Polynesian influences in some islands. Traditional customs vary by island. Shell money is still used ceremonially.
