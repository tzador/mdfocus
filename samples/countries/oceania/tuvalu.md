## History

Polynesians settled Tuvalu about 3,000 years ago. Britain colonized as the Ellice Islands. Tuvalu separated from Kiribati (Gilbert Islands) and gained independence in 1978.

## Geography

Tuvalu consists of nine islands, all coral atolls. No point is higher than 4.6 meters. The country is one of the most vulnerable to climate change.

## Economy

Tuvalu has few resources. Major income comes from the .tv internet domain, fishing licenses, and foreign aid. Climate change threatens the country's existence.

## Culture

Tuvaluan culture is traditional Polynesian. Communal living, traditional dancing (fatele), and Christianity are central.
