## Geography

Samoa consists of two main islands (Upolu and Savai'i) and smaller islands. The terrain is volcanic with lush tropical vegetation. The climate is tropical with cyclone risk.

## Economy

Tourism, remittances, agriculture (taro, coconut), and fishing drive the economy. Many Samoans live overseas, particularly in New Zealand.

## Culture

Fa'a Samoa (the Samoan Way) governs traditional life. Extended family (aiga) and village chiefs (matai) are central. Tattooing and traditional dance are distinctive.
