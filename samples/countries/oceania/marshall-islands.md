## History

Micronesians settled the islands over 2,000 years ago. Germany, then Japan (under League mandate), controlled them before US trusteeship after WWII. The US conducted 67 nuclear tests at Bikini and Enewetak atolls (1946-1958). Independence came in 1986 under free association with the US.

## Geography

The Marshall Islands comprise 29 coral atolls and 5 islands. Terrain is low-lying and vulnerable to climate change. The lagoons are extensive.

## Economy

The economy depends on US compact payments, fishing licenses, and a ship registry. Agriculture is limited. Nuclear testing legacy continues to affect some atolls.

## Culture

Marshallese culture values traditional navigation and canoe-building. Christianity is the main religion. The nuclear legacy has shaped national identity.
