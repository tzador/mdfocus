## History

Human habitation dates back 50,000 years. German and British colonies merged under Australian control. Independence came on September 16, 1975. Bougainville had a separatist conflict; a 2019 referendum favored independence.

## Geography

PNG is mostly mountainous rainforest, with some of the world's least explored terrain. Active volcanoes, coral reefs, and diverse ecosystems characterize the landscape.

## Economy

Mining (gold, copper) and natural gas dominate exports. Most of the population practices subsistence agriculture. Development challenges include geography and governance.
