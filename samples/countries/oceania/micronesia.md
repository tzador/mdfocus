**The Federated States of Micronesia** (FSM) is an island nation in the western Pacific. Comprising four states across 607 islands, it has a Compact of Free Association with the United States.

## History

The islands were settled by Micronesians over 4,000 years ago. Nan Madol on Pohnpei is an ancient megalithic city. Spain, Germany, Japan, and the US successively controlled the islands. Independence under free association came in 1986.

## Geography

FSM consists of four states: Yap, Chuuk, Pohnpei, and Kosrae. The islands are high volcanic and low coral atolls. The climate is tropical. Chuuk Lagoon has significant WWII wreck diving.

## Economy

The economy depends heavily on US compact funding. Fishing and subsistence agriculture are important. Tourism, especially diving, has potential.

## Culture

Each state has distinct cultures and languages. Traditional navigation, yam cultivation, and stone money (Yap) are notable. Christianity is widespread but traditional practices continue.
