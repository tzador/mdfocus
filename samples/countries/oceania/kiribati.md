## History

Micronesians settled the islands thousands of years ago. Britain colonized as the Gilbert Islands. Independence came on July 12, 1979. The Battle of Tarawa (1943) was a significant WWII Pacific engagement.

## Geography

Kiribati consists of 33 atolls and reef islands in three groups spanning 3,441 km of ocean. Most land is only a few meters above sea level. Climate change and sea level rise are existential threats.

## Economy

Kiribati is extremely remote and resource-limited. Fishing licenses, remittances, and foreign aid support the economy. Copra is a minor export.

## Culture

I-Kiribati culture is traditional Micronesian. Communal living and fishing are central. Traditional dance and navigation are valued. Christianity is the dominant religion.
