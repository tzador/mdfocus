## History

Tonga was unified as a kingdom in 1845 and became a British protectorate (1900-1970) while maintaining independence. It is the only Pacific monarchy. King George Tupou V introduced democratic reforms in 2010.

## Geography

Tonga consists of 169 islands (36 inhabited) in three main groups. The islands are low coral atolls and volcanic peaks. The 2022 Hunga Tonga eruption was one of the most powerful ever recorded.

## Economy

Remittances, agriculture, fishing, and tourism drive the economy. Squash exports to Japan are notable. The 2022 eruption caused significant damage.

## Culture

Tongan culture is traditional Polynesian with strong Christian influence. Kava ceremonies, traditional dancing, and respect for the monarchy are central.
