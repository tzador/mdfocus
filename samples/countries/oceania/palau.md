## History

Micronesians settled Palau over 3,000 years ago. Spain, Germany, Japan, and the US successively controlled the islands. Independence under free association came in 1994.

## Geography

Palau consists of about 340 islands. The Rock Islands, with their distinctive mushroom shapes, are a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Marine biodiversity is exceptional.

## Economy

Tourism, especially diving, dominates. Fishing and subsistence agriculture are important. US compact payments support the budget.

## Culture

Palauan culture maintains traditional clan systems and land tenure. The bai (meeting house) is architecturally distinctive. Storycarvings depict legends and history.
