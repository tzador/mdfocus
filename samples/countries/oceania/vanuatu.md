## Geography

Vanuatu is an archipelago of about 83 islands stretching 1,300 km. The terrain is volcanic, with active volcanoes on several islands. Earthquakes and cyclones are risks.

## Economy

Tourism, agriculture (copra, kava, beef), and fishing drive the economy. Citizenship by investment is growing. The country is frequently affected by natural disasters.

## Culture

Vanuatu has over 100 languages for its small population. Land diving on Pentecost Island inspired bungee jumping. Kastom (traditional culture) remains strong alongside Christianity.
