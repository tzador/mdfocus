**Nauru**, officially the **Republic of Nauru**, is the world's third-smallest country by area and smallest island nation. Once wealthy from phosphate mining, it exemplifies resource curse and boom-bust cycles.

## History

Micronesians and Polynesians settled Nauru. Germany colonized in 1888, then Australia administered after WWI. Phosphate mining created enormous wealth. Independence came on January 31, 1968.

## Geography

Nauru is a single raised coral island. Phosphate mining has stripped the interior, leaving an environmental wasteland. The climate is tropical.

## Economy

Phosphate was exhausted by 2000, leaving an environmental and economic disaster. The country now hosts Australian offshore immigration processing and relies on foreign aid.

## Culture

Nauruan culture is Micronesian. The traditional weight-lifting sport has historical significance. The population has high obesity rates due to lifestyle changes.
