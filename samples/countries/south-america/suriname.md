## History

Indigenous peoples inhabited the region. The Dutch colonized, bringing enslaved Africans and later indentured workers from India, Indonesia, and China. Independence came on November 25, 1975. Military coups and economic crisis marked later decades.

## Geography

Suriname is mostly tropical rainforest, with over 90% forest cover. The coastal plain is where most people live. The country has significant biodiversity.

## Economy

Suriname's economy depends on bauxite, oil, and gold mining. Agriculture and ecotourism have potential. Economic management has been troubled.

## Culture

Suriname is exceptionally diverse: Hindustanis, Creoles, Javanese, Maroons (descendants of escaped slaves), and indigenous peoples coexist. This diversity is reflected in food, languages, and traditions.
