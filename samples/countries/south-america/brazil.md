## History

Brazil was a Portuguese colony from 1500, the only South American country colonized by Portugal. Independence came in 1822 under Emperor Pedro I. The empire lasted until 1889. Military dictatorship (1964-1985) was followed by democratic restoration. Political polarization has increased recently.

## Geography

Brazil spans nearly half of South America, with the Amazon basin covering most of the north. The country has extensive Atlantic coastline, the Pantanal wetland, and the Atlantic Forest. Climate ranges from tropical to subtropical.

## Economy

Brazil has Latin America's largest economy, diversified across agriculture (soybeans, coffee, beef), industry, and services. The country is a leading commodity exporter. Inequality remains high.
