## History

The region was disputed between Spain and Portugal. Independence came in 1828 as a buffer state. Uruguay was a welfare state pioneer in the early 20th century. Military dictatorship (1973-1985) was followed by democratic restoration.

## Economy

Uruguay has a stable, service-oriented economy. Agriculture (beef, wool, soybeans) is important. The country is a regional financial center. Tourism (Punta del Este) is significant.

## Culture

Uruguayan culture shares much with Argentina: mate drinking, asado, tango, and football passion. The country legalized marijuana first in the world and has progressive social policies. Candombe drumming is a distinctive tradition.
